Radhe Fabrication Rescues an Industrial Project in Vatva GIDC, Ahmedabad

Overview

In the heart of Ahmedabad’s industrial hub, Vatva GIDC, a manufacturing unit hired a budget fabricator to install a new industrial shed, railings, and custom metal fixtures. Midway through, the work stopped: substandard scrap metal had been used, and the inexperienced team walked off the job—leaving the factory stalled and unsafe.

The Client

A large factory in Vatva GIDC needed a turnkey metal-work solution for a new production wing. Their timeline was tight, and safety was critical.

Challenges

  • Poor-quality materials (scrap steel) jeopardizing structural integrity
  • Incomplete work left site unsafe and operations halted.
  • Inexperienced crew unable to handle large, complex assemblies.
  • Urgent timeline—every day of delay meant revenue lost.

Solutions

  • Immediate Site Assessment: Radhe Fabrication’s senior engineers surveyed all faulty structures.
  • Dismantling & Cleanup: Safely removed substandard frames and cleared the site.
  • Premium Materials: Supplied weather-resistant, industrial-grade steel sourced locally in Ahmedabad.
  • Custom Design & Fabrication: Re-engineered the shed, railings, and fixtures to meet exact specs.
