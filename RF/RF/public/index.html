About Us

Radhe Fabrication is a leading name in the field of metal fabrication with over 35 years of industry experience. We specialize in manufacturing a wide range of fabricated products such as compounding gates, fiber sheds, pre-coated sheds, shine boards and hoardings, railings, and katheda structures. Our extensive experience has enabled us to successfully complete numerous projects across Gujarat, consistently delivering high-quality results that align with our clients’ expectations and budgets.

In a city as bustling as Ahmedabad, metalwork is everywhere – from stair railings to industrial frames. A well-designed railing must blend safety with contemporary aesthetics. Yet despite the demand, finding truly reliable Fabrication in Ahmedabad is not easy. Many homeowners end up googling “metal fabrication near me” only to see dozens of local workshops, but proximity doesn’t guarantee quality. How do you pick a fabricator who will deliver strong, durable structures instead of cutting corners? In this blog, we’ll break down what to look for in Ahmedabad’s fabrication market and explain why one company – Radhe Fabrication – consistently stands out.
